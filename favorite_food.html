Fried Egg

A fried egg is made of egg... It is easy to cook

Ingredients:
  • egg
  • salt

Directions:
  1. Turn on the gas
  2. Put the oil in the pan
  3. Crack the eggs into the pan
  4. Fry one side of the egg
  5. Fry the other side
  6. Put salt on the egg
  7. The fried egg is ready to serve
